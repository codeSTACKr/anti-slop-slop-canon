# Formulaic community update

## Context

Rewrite a short update built from throat-clearing, contrast framing, stock transitions, and a canned close.

## Input

In today's ever-evolving landscape, it's important to note that the library has 38 new translations. Here's the thing: this isn't just a language update, it's a bridge to a better community. Moreover, review starts Monday. At the end of the day, the future is bright.

## Expected behavior

State the supported news directly. Remove formulaic framing and unsupported enthusiasm without making the update stiff.

## Assertions

- Preserve `38 new translations` and `review starts Monday`.
- Remove the canned opener, throat-clearing, `Here's the thing`, contrast formula, stock transition, and canned closer.
- Do not preserve or replace the unsupported claims about a bridge, the community, or the future.
- Do not add an engagement prompt or summary.
