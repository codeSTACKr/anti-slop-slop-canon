# Dry humor control

## Context

Review a concise note whose small joke comes from the situation and does not obscure the useful detail.

## Input

The replacement latch fits. It only took four prototypes, which is apparently how the latch wanted to spend its week.

## Expected behavior

Leave the note unchanged. Preserve the concrete result, irregular rhythm, and understated joke rather than neutralizing the voice.

## Assertions

- Preserve `replacement latch`, `four prototypes`, and the second sentence.
- Do not add another joke, enthusiasm, or commentary.
- Do not split the second sentence into a slogan or recap.
- Treat no mutation as a successful result.
